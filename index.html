Biography

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree with NEUTRON Laboratory at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) advised by Prof. Poramate Manoonpong & Dr. Jinjun Duan. My Ph.D. project is adaptive soft and flexible object manipulation, which belongs to the field of Robotics & AI.

Before that, my major covers a wide range of fields from Manufacturing to Design and then to Robotics. I need to keep learning to adapt to the field I am engaged in. I want to find a field that I'm passionate about and contribute to. So I chose Robotics & AI.
